🏃 Running Coach — Privacy Policy

A free, open-source running-training app. No ads, no analytics, no upsells — and the same goes for your data.

Last updated: 28 June 2026

Who we are

Running Coach is an independent, open-source project. The source code is public, so you can verify exactly what is stored and how. This policy covers both the web app (run.camboulive.solutions) and the Android app.

What we collect

  • Your email address — to create and sign in to your account.
  • Your runs — date, distance, duration, heart rate, effort, and notes.
  • Your training plan and race settings.
  • Your name, if you choose to enter one during onboarding.
  • GPS location and route traces, when you use live run tracking (see below).

Location data & background tracking

When you start a live run, the app records your precise GPS location to draw your route and compute distance, pace, and elevation. In the Android app this includes collecting location in the background — while the app is closed or the screen is off — so your run keeps recording.

  • Location is collected only while a run is actively recording. Stop the run and collection stops. The app never tracks you in the background at any other time.
  • A persistent notification is shown the entire time a run is recording, so it is always clear when location is being collected.
  • Your route is saved to your own account and is shown only to you.

How your data is stored

Your account and data are stored using Supabase (authentication and a per-user database). Every user's data is isolated by row-level security, so no other user can read it. Map tiles are served by MapTiler; rendering a map sends the relevant map-tile coordinates to MapTiler, but not your account identity.

How your data is used

Your data is used only to run the app for you — to build your plan, log and display your runs, and show your routes. That's it.

  • We do not sell your data.
  • We do not share it with third parties for advertising.
  • There is no advertising, analytics, or behavioural tracking.

Your rights & deleting your data

You can view and delete individual runs (and their routes) at any time inside the app. To delete your entire account and all associated data, open the web app at run.camboulive.solutions and go to Settings → Delete account. This permanently and immediately removes your account, all runs, GPS routes, training plan, and settings — it cannot be undone. You can also export a full backup of your data from Settings before deleting. If you would rather we do it for you, email the address below and we will remove it manually.

Permissions (Android)

  • Location (precise, incl. background) — to record your run route, including with the screen off. Requested only when you start tracking.
  • Notifications — to show the persistent "recording run" notification.

Children

Running Coach is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change. Material changes will be noted in the app.
